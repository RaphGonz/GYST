The Foundation Sprint runs four steps in sequence. Each step must complete before the next begins.

**Step 1: The Basics**
Lock in your target customer, core problem, founder advantages, and the initial competitor list. The AI researches competitor profiles and validates that the pain is real, critical, and frequent.

**Step 2: Differentiation**
Rate yourself and all competitors on bipolar axes. Identify two axes where you can dominate the top-right quadrant. Produce a 2x2 positioning matrix and a three-phrase mini-manifesto.

**Step 3: Approaches**
Evaluate multiple solution approaches through four lenses: customer vision, money vision, pragmatic vision, and growth vision. The AI generates options filtered by your specific founder capacity and insight. You commit to one approach.

**Step 4: Final Hypothesis**
Formulate the X/Y/Z/W/U/V hypothesis: "If we help X solve Y with Z, they will choose us over W because we are U and V." Produce HYPOTHESIS.md, SPRINT.md, POSITIONING.md, and COMPETITORS.md.
</steps>
